About the App

The Smart Student Card Appointment System is a user-friendly platform designed to streamline and simplify the process of booking student card collection appointments. It eliminates the need for long queues and confusion by allowing students to schedule their appointments in advance, ensuring better organization and efficient service. The system also allows students to submit feedback and suggestions to help improve campus services, making it a valuable communication tool between students and administrators. This project was developed by a dedicated team of third-year Computer Science students Uviwe Sotyato , Nonjabulo Mtshali , Khululekani Zenani , Shrewdness Maluleke and Sinazo Zumana .
